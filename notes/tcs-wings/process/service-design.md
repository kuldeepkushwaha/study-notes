# Service Design

## What is service design?

Service is process of improving customer experience through the research , developing ideas and testing experience.

Research - It begin by taking to everyone who are affected by this service
Develop ideas - IT works with stakeholders to create a variety of ideas that will improve the service
Testing - IT picks the most appealing idea and build prototypes.

Purpose of service design:
- To ensure that the new or changed service are designed to meet the changing  requirement of business, for introduction into live environment.

## Five aspect of service design

STAMP
1) Solution 
2) Tool
3) Architecture
4) Measurement system and Metrics
5) Designing the process needed
