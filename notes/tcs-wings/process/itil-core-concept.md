# ITIL Core Concept

## Type of services

1) Core Services
2) Enabling Services
3) Enhancing Services

-> Core Services: 
 It is a service that must be there for any value to be delivered
-> Enabling Service
It is a service that must be place for a core service to be delivered.
-> Enhancing Services:
Service that are added to  the core services to make it more enticing to the customer are called Enhancing Services

---

## Service Asset

Service asset consist  of capablities and resources that the organizational use to deliver the value in the form of service

Type of service provider:
Internal service provider- for every domain they have there own IT team manage internally, and also they can be internally centralized.
Extranal service provider - act as intermediate between customer and company which provide service. let say example tcs proviode service to db,passport kendr etc.

## Key param of ITIL

Process - a structural set of activies  designed to accomplish  a specify objective
Function - team or group of people  and tools they use to  carry out the process and acitivty
role - Played by individual to within function  when performing process.
