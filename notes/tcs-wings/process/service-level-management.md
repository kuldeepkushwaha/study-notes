# Service level management

## Six stage of service level management

1) SLA preparation and agreement
2) service level monitoring
3) service level reporting 
4) service level reviewing
5) service level improvement and program
6) SLA maintenance.

---

## Key terms of sla

1) Service credit - A credit paid by vendor to client for missing an sla (service default)
2) Earn back - The ability of the vendor not have to pay the service credit
