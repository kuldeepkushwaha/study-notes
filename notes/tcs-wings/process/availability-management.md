# Availability management

## Defination

Availability Management  provide us with a framework where the availability  needs  of the business is understood first and is then delivered

Purpose:
 To ensure the level of availability delivered in all IT services meets the agreed  availability  needs of the business.

Proactive availability management
Reactive availability management
