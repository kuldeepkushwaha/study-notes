# Demand Management

## Demand Management play's key role in Service management

1) Anticipate future demand
2) Align suppy with demand
3)  Provide just enough capacity for server to meet the agreed service levels .
    a) Too much capacity is a wasted cost
    b) Too little capacity puts service at risk
