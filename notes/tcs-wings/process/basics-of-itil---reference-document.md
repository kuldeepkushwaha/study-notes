## ITSM

Information technology and service management.

Defination: ITSM is set of organizational capablities to provide the value to the customer throught services.

What is Service:  Service is a mean of delivering the value to the customer by faciliating the outcomes that the customer want to achieve without the ownership of specfic cost and risk.

## ITIL

Information technology and infrastructure library.
Defination : ITIL is a set of pratices for IT service management that focus on alignment the IT service with the needs of business.

It is framework which help below:
-> Increase the productivity
-> Lower IT cost
-> Improving service

History of ITIL:
Orginally developed in 1980's by UK Goverment agency  - CCTA( forerunner of OGC)
* Central computer telecommunication agency.
* Office of goverment commerce

Since Jan 2014 ITIL is owned and managed by axelos

Version:
1980's - Infrastructure management grew into more that 30 books cover specfic aspect of IT service and provision (version 1)
2001 - Intial version was reviesed and  replace by 9 books  focus on service delivery and support.(version 2)
2007 - 5 core book focus on service lifecycle approach (version 3)
2011 - Improved clarity and consistency (version 4)
