## Four Ps form of Strategy

1) Perspective - vision and direction of organization 
2) Postion - describes how the service providers intent to compete with other service provider in the market
3) Plan - Describe how service provider will transition from current state to their desire state.
4) Pattern - Describes on going repeatable action  that a service provider will have to preform consistently in order to continue to meet its strategic object.
